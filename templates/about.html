About KavyaGuard

KavyaGuard is an AI-powered system built to detect hate speech and offensive content in Kannada text. Our goal is to help create a safer online environment by identifying harmful or inappropriate language using modern Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques.

The platform is simple, fast, and designed to support students, developers, and content moderators in promoting responsible digital communication.
